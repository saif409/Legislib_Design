Cited in and Cited By

Legal library includes a list of references under each source. Next to each document there is a "cited by" and a “cited in” link. Clicking on this link shows Legal library's citation analysis.

  • Cited In lists which cases and laws are cited in a document
  • Cited By lists which cases or laws have cited a specific document
